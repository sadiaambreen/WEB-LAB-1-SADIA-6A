    Babar Azam, is a Pakistani international cricketer who captains Pakistan in all formats. He captains Karachi Kings in the PSL and captains Central Punjab in domestic cricket. In April 2021, he achieved the number one spot in the ICC rankings for ODI batsmen.

  • Sahibzada Mohammad Shahid Khan Afridi, known as Shahid Afridi, is a former Pakistani cricketer and captain of the Pakistan national cricket team.
